About Barbara Sharon

I believe that language learning should be an immersive and enjoyable experience. I am committed to helping you achieve fluency in Portuguese, while also gaining cultural insights that will broaden your horizons.

My courses are designed to meet the needs of learners at all levels, from beginners to advanced speakers. I offer a variety of classes, including group lessons, private tutoring, and online courses. The dynamic and vibrant learning environment will inspire you to reach your full potential as a language learner.
