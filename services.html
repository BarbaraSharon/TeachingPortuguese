Learning Options

Portuguese Intermediate Course

Immerse yourself in the world of Portuguese with my specially designed 10-week Intermediate -level group sessions. My classes are a fun and vibrant learning environment. Embark on this journey to take your Portuguese language skills to the next level.

Private Session

Experience customised one-to-one sessions in either Portuguese or English, or you can share the cost of private lessons with two of your friends or family members. This way, you can enjoy a personalised learning experience while also benefiting from group learning.

Portuguese Beginner Course

Immerse yourself in the world of Portuguese with my specially designed 10-week Beginner-level group sessions. My classes are a fun and vibrant learning environment. Embark on this journey to take your Portuguese language skills to the next level.

Speaking Club

Engaging sessions focusing on speaking, listening, and expanding your vocabulary, using innovative teaching methods such as task-based learning, NLP, and mind mapping. But it doesn't stop there. I incorporate cooking, painting, music, and body language, into the learning process to make it fun and engaging.

As a certified and qualified Portuguese teacher and mental health counsellor, I blend language education with effective counselling techniques to create an immersive and empowering learning experience.
