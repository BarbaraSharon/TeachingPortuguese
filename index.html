Portuguese Tutoring & Language Learning
Gold Coast, Queensland

Welcome to Portuguese Tutoring & Language Learning with Barbara Sharon, your local expert for learning Portuguese on the Gold Coast, Queensland, Australia. I offer dynamic, well-structured Portuguese language courses, private tutoring, and group classes designed to help you achieve fluency with confidence.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Languages and Literature from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ) and am a TESOL-certified instructor. In addition to Portuguese and English, I speak Italian and Spanish, which allows me to anticipate challenges, clarify differences, and make learning smoother for my students.

I am also a counsellor and currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Educational Studies and Behaviour Management. This unique background helps me create a supportive and motivating environment where every student can thrive.

My lessons are carefully structured to combine grammar, vocabulary, conversation, and cultural insights, so you learn not just the language, but how to use it naturally in real life. Whether you are on the Gold Coast, Queensland, or anywhere in the World, I am here to help you succeed in your Portuguese learning journey.
